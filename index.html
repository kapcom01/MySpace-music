🎵 Music Discovery Hub

Extract song metadata from MySpace profiles and discover them on official streaming platforms.

Step 1 — Provide a MySpace profile URL

Example: https://myspace.com/myspace/music/songs

Step 2 — Discovered songs

    About this tool

    • 🔒 Privacy-first: all processing happens in your browser.
    • ⚖️ Legal & ethical: links only to official streaming services.
    • 🚫 No download button — this is a discovery tool, not a downloader.
    • 🌐 Uses allorigins.win as a public CORS proxy.
